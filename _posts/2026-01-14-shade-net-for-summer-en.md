When summer heat increases in Gujarat, a shade net helps protect your roses. Keep these points in mind:

- Use **50% shade net or less**.
- **White shade net** is preferable as it reflects heat better.

**Why not more than 50%?** More than 50% shade net will reduce temperature, but it will also reduce light intensity. Roses need 50,000 lux light intensity for good growth and blooming.
